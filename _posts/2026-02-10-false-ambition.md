You cannot do the things you want because did not commit to doing things that will get you there. 

False ambition and romanticizing the process instead of being in it. When chasing everything, you commit to nothing. Because if you do you cannot chase everything. But, committing to nothing ultimately yeilds nothing.

Resigning into a life of nothing at all. Committments as root instead of chains. Caught between true desires and lies telling yourself. Belive less in potential and more in progress everyday.

And Jung said
> "Fear of life. Man isn't born with value but creates his value".  

What if you fail? 
What if you don't even try?

We are pleasure seeking animals. Make sure the pleasures you choose now don't ruin the pleasures you could enjoy in your future.
